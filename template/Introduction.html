Introduction


Background

The Vocational Training Council (VTC) will conduct regular manpower surveys and publish reports for 24 major industry sectors to assess their manpower demands and supplies.
According to this manpower survey, it is a quantitative survey conducted once every 4 years to collect information of employees. In this 4-year cycle two manpower updates will be conducted through focus group meeting with industry representatives and data analytics of major recruitment platforms like CPJobs and CTgoodjob.


Project Objectives

We need to enhance the efficiency of the operation efficiency of the Manpower Survey conducted by the HQIP of VTC, including enhancing the data collection, cleaning, modeling, and classification job advertisements. We need to retrieve, clean, and classify unstructured job advertisements into corresponding Principal Job Titles of an industry.


Business Requirements

Use the Data Science Technology to improve the efficiency and accuracy of job advertisements classification for the Manpower Survey conducted by HQIP of VTC and looking for a data analytic solution to simplify the task and enhance its accuracy.

title, job_code, education_level, working_experience, part_time, full_time.
